Korean Tofu Stew

Description

This is my pescatarian version of sundubu-jjigae, a classic Korean comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 block or tube silken tofu
  • 2 tbsp gochugaru
  • 4-6 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 4 cups kombu dashi
  • 1 tsp instant bonito dashi powder
  • 1 tsp mushroom powder
  • Olive or vegetable oil
  • 1 pkg Beech mushrooms, end cut off
  • 5 leaves napa cabbage, cut into medium pieces
  • One beaten egg
  • Chopped scallion for garnish
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Drizzle of sesame oil

Directions

  1. Heat up generous amount of oil. When hot, put in gochugaru and garlic. Stir until fragrant, avoid burning.
  2. Add soy sauce and fish sauce. Let cook for 2 min. Add dashi along with bonito powder and mushroom powder. Simmer for 5 min.
  3. Add silken tofu, mushrooms, and cabbage. Simmer for 5 min.
  4. Slowly pour in egg and let it form into ribbons. Cook for 1 min.
  5. Turn off heat and finish with salt, pepper, and drizzle of sesame oil to taste. Garnish with scallion.
