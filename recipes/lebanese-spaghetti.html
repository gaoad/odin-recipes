Lebanese Spaghetti with Zucchini

Description

An easy pasta recipe with a yogurt-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 zucchini, cut into quarter chunks
  • 1 pack spaghetti
  • 1 cup plain whole milk yogurt (not greek)
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • Handful of parsley leaves, finely chopped
  • Handful of basil, chiffonade cut
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Put zucchini on baking sheet and add olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast zucchini at 425F until tender.
  2. Boil spaghetti. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat up generous amount of olive oil. Add parsley and garlic, saute until fragrant, without burning.
  4. Turn heat down to low and add yogurt. Stir to combine.
  5. Mix in spaghetti, basil, roasted zucchini, and generous salt and pepper.
